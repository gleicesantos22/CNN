A baby who was born healthy, with no signs of brain injury, had her life permanently shattered after an episode of extreme violence committed by her own father when she was just three months old.

Christa was born without complications, required no medical devices, and showed normal development for her age.

According to family members, there was no prior history of neurological or health issues.

That reality, however, was irreversibly destroyed after what investigators describe as an act of extreme injustice with devastating consequences.

Shortly after her parents separated, Christa’s father was left alone with her for the first time.

On the night of December 20, witnesses say he left the baby alone at home to go to a bar.

Reports indicate he returned hours later intoxicated and emotionally unstable.

According to the investigation, around 11 p.m., the man sexually assaulted his own daughter.

The violence did not stop there.

Christa was brutally beaten with extreme force, triggering a series of seizures that continued throughout the night.

The child did not receive medical care until the following day.

When she arrived at the hospital, doctors determined that she had suffered a stroke.

Shortly afterward, Christa went into cardiac arrest and lost all brain function.

Today, Christa remains in critical condition, completely dependent on continuous life support.

Specialists say her condition is consistent with severe trauma caused by physical abuse.

A child who should be experiencing discovery and growth now faces a reality defined by extreme limitations and constant suffering.

Doctors say that Christa’s only chance of survival depends on a critical, life-saving surgery estimated to cost approximately $70,000.

The procedure is being described as a medical miracle, the only intervention capable of keeping Christa alive and preventing permanent damage to her body.

Without health insurance and without effective government assistance, Christa’s family says they have reached their absolute limit.

Every resource has been exhausted. Personal belongings have been sold.

There are no savings left, no alternatives, only time, which is now running out.

Christa’s case goes beyond the record of an extreme act of violence.

It has become an urgent call for public action.

Physicians involved in her care warn that the window for intervention is critical and could close at any moment.

“If the surgery is not performed within the next days, unfortunately, there will be no chance of survival, ” doctors say.

Every dollar donated, every share, and every gesture of support represents a real chance to give Christa back the life that was brutally taken from her and pay for legal actions agains the ''Father''.

This is your chance to save the life of Christa.
